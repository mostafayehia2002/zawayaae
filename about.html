About Us

Amzan Middle East LLC was established in 2004 and registered in accordance to the requirements of United Arab Emirates laws. In our decade of existence, we have grown in to one of the leading interior decorations company. We are proud to have been trusted by many companies of local and international repute for providing high quality works.

The office and factory are located in AL Quoz Industrial area-1 in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. The factory has got an area of 30,000 square feet with fully equipped in-house facilities. We have registered our stamp of class by executing varies prestigious projects in varied domain, such as manufacturing, real estate, banking, retail, health and sports in the UAE. With a decade of experience behind us in the industry, we have carved a niche for ourselves one of the reputed company in the field offering a full spectrum of specialized professional services that include from designing to the installation.

Dealing with prestigious projects on a turn-key basis and working with the consultants, Designers, Architects and Engineers of reputed organizations has helped us to be a confident and competent presence in the market in setting new standards of quality within the industry. Addressing the dealing challenges effectively and innovatively with our diversified expertise; we have been successful in winning and executing prestigious projects. With the advancement in technology, we have been constantly striving hard to reach greater heights through innovation and sustainable progressive growth and in the process provide our valuable customers a comprehensive solution for their requirements.
